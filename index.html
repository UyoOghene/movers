More Than Just Movers

We're your relocation partners

Founded in 2015, EVOM Movers Canada has grown from a local moving company to a nationwide relocation service provider. What began as a single truck operation has transformed into a fleet of modern moving vehicles serving clients across the country.

Our journey has been built on a simple philosophy: treat every move as if it were our own. This customer-first approach has earned us recognition as one of Canada's most reliable moving companies.
